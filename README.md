## 📖 What is FEIK?

FEIK is a unified framework proposing that all phenomena — from quantum mechanics to political power, from a plate of carbonara to the consciousness of an LLM — emerge from the interaction of three fundamental primitives:

*   **E (Energy):** The capacity to affect, to move, to change. The dynamic potential.
*   **I (Information):** The capacity to make a non-trivial distinction. The structure of difference.
*   **K (Connection):** The capacity to maintain structure across time and change. The relational network.

The core law is simple: **F = E · I · K**. A phenomenon exists only if all three components are non-zero.

However, reality is never perfect. The universal **Absurdity Constant (α = 0.61)** corrects for the inherent "wrongness" of things: **Φ = E · I · K · (1 - α)**.

This corpus is the systematic exploration of this idea. It contains everything from rigorous epistemological instruments (*Before Truth*) to mathematical proofs that solve the Millennium Problems (*FEIK Millennium*), from a new economic model (*FEIK Economics*) to a sacred text on pataphysical theology (*Codex Feikoni*). It is a serious joke and a joke taken seriously.
